# Styled in Motion — Creator Quickstart Guide

Welcome to Styled in Motion! This guide walks you through everything you need to know to start sharing your looks, growing your audience, and earning commissions.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

Open the app and tap **Creator Login**. On the **Create Account** tab, enter your name, email, and a password (at least 6 characters). Tap **Create Account**.

### 2. Set Up Your Username

After signing up, you'll choose a unique username. This is your public handle — shoppers will find you by it. Type something memorable, and the app will check availability in real time. If it's taken, tap one of the suggested alternatives.

### 3. Connect Your Socials

Link your Instagram and TikTok by entering your handles. Toggle each platform on or off. You can always update these later in your profile. Tap **Save & Continue** when you're ready, or **Skip for now** to set them up later.

---

## Your Home Tab

Your home screen is your personal dashboard. At a glance you'll see:

- A grid of all your published looks
- Your total stats: **Looks**, **Items**, **Clicks**, and **Followers**

Tap any look to view, share, edit, or archive it.

---

## Creating a Look

This is where the magic happens. Tap the **Create** tab to start.

### Step 1 — Upload a Cover Photo

Pick a photo from your camera roll or snap a new one. Use the built-in editor to crop, rotate, or adjust brightness until it's perfect.

### Step 2 — Add Your Items

Add each item in the outfit:

- **Paste a product link** — the app auto-fills the item name, brand, price, and photo for you.
- Or add details manually: pick a category (Top, Pants, Dress, Shoes, Bag, Jewelry, Accessory, Outerwear, Other), enter the name, brand, and price.
- Optionally add an **alternative item** (a similar product at a different price point — your audience will love the options).

Repeat for every piece in the look.

### Step 3 — Choose a Layout

Pick from 4 templates that set the vibe for your look:

| Layout | Style |
|---|---|
| **Clean Grid** | Minimal white space, editorial feel |
| **Minimal Luxury** | Centered photo, items below |
| **Cozy Neutral** | Warm beige, stacked layout |
| **Bold Influencer** | High contrast, full-width |

### Step 4 — Add a Title & Caption

Give your look a title and write (or auto-generate) a caption. Add hashtags and tags so shoppers can discover you.

### Step 5 — Publish & Share

Hit publish, then share your look everywhere:

- **Instagram Post** — Saves all photos to your camera roll and copies a ready-to-paste caption with item names, prices, and hashtags.
- **Instagram Story** — Saves the cover photo and copies a shareable link for the link sticker.
- **TikTok** — Copies a link to your clipboard to paste in your bio or comments.
- **General Share** — Send via Messages, WhatsApp, email, or any other app.

## The Shop Tab

Your Shop tab is your storefront and management hub.

### Views

Switch between four views at the top:

- **Looks** — All your published looks in a grid. Tap a look to see full details, share it again, edit, archive, or delete it. Long-press to quickly rename.
- **Items** — Every item across all your looks.
- **Closet** — Your personal item inventory. Add standalone items here (even outside of a look) so you can reuse them later.
- **Archives** — Hidden looks and items. Archive instead of deleting if you might want them back.

### Your Shop Link

At the top of the Shop tab you'll see your personal shop link:
**app.styledinmotion.app/yourusername**

Copy and paste this anywhere — your Instagram bio, TikTok profile, Linktree, or website — so shoppers can browse all your looks in one place.

### Search

Use the search bar to find any look or item by title, caption, item name, or brand.

---

## Your Profile

Tap the **Profile** tab to set up your creator brand.

### Basics

- **Profile Photo** — Tap your avatar to pick a new photo.
- **Username** — Tap to edit. The app checks availability in real time and suggests alternatives if taken.
- **Bio** — A short description shoppers see when they visit your profile.
- **Location** — Helps shoppers discover creators in their area.

### Connected Platforms

Add your Instagram and TikTok handles. Toggle each one on so they show on your public profile.

### Primary Platform

Choose which platform you consider your main one (Instagram or TikTok). The app will try to auto-fetch your follower count from this platform first.

### Follower Count & Tier

Your total social followers determine your tier and commission rate:

| Tier | Followers | You Keep |
|---|---|---|
| Nano | Under 1,000 | 99% |
| Micro | 1,001 – 5,000 | 98% |
| Mid-Tier | 5,001 – 10,000 | 95% |
| Macro | 10,001 – 15,000 | 90% |
| Mega | 15,000+ | 85% |

The app auto-fetches your count if your profile is public. You can also enter it manually.

### Caption Settings

Customize how auto-generated captions work:

- **Style**: Casual, Professional, or Minimal
- **Include Hashtags**: Auto-adds tags like #ootd and #fashion
- **Include Prices**: Shows item prices right in the caption

## Analytics

Tap the **Stats** tab to see how you're doing.

### Key Metrics

- **Look Views** — Total times your looks were seen
- **Item Clicks** — How many times shoppers tapped to shop an item
- **Followers** — Your in-app follower count
- **Looks Posted** — Total looks you've published

Toggle between **Last 30 Days** and **All Time**.

### How Shoppers Find You

A breakdown showing whether views come from:

- **Following Feed** — Your followers' home screens
- **Discover** — The trending/explore section
- **Profile** — Direct visits to your profile
- **Search** — Keyword searches

### Top Looks

Your best-performing looks ranked by views, with click counts so you know which items drive the most engagement.

### Follower Growth

A growth chart showing your follower trend over time. Toggle between Instagram and TikTok to track each platform.

## Tips for Success

1. **Add product links** — Looks with shoppable links get significantly more engagement. Paste the URL and let the app auto-fill the details.

2. **Offer alternatives** — Adding a budget-friendly or luxury alternative to items gives shoppers more reasons to click.

3. **Share everywhere** — Use the one-tap share for Instagram, Stories, and TikTok every time you publish. The app formats everything for you.

4. **Keep your shop link visible** — Put your shop link (app.styledinmotion.app/yourusername) in every bio and profile you have.

5. **Post consistently** — Creators who post 2–3 looks per week grow their followers fastest.

6. **Use hashtags** — Turn on auto-hashtags in your profile settings so every caption includes relevant tags.

7. **Pick the right layout** — Match the layout to the content. Clean Grid works great for multi-item outfits; Bold Influencer is perfect for statement pieces.

8. **Check your stats** — Visit the Analytics tab weekly. See what's working and do more of it.
